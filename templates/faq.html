Below are a few FAQ's for your information. For any additional questions not listed please just get in contact with me via the contact form for a free consultation.


What should I wear?

Please wear comfortable shoes and clothing that you can actively run in and that does not restrict movement.

Do I need to bring any equipment?

Not at all. All equipment is provided in the gym. All I ask is that you wear some comfortable clothes and shoes and to bring a positive attitude.

What does personal training involve?

As explained sessions will be tailored to you at our first session we will go over and create a workout plan that works for you with your goals, achieveable challenges. Each session will always include stretching, a warming up and cooling off period, this is to ensure your body is ready for exercise and to not shock your system.

How long is a session?

Generally a training session will last 1 hour but it can vary for each individual and their workout plan

Where do we train?

We can train in the gym, outside in the good outdoors or at other venues.

When will I see results?

It is difficult to say. Each individual is different and will have their own lifestyle, workout goals, challenges and different starting points. Seeing physical results can also vary depending on current eating habits, biology and exercise habits. I can say that you will likely feel the results first: from reps getting easier to feeling a lot happier doing exercise.I can say that if you put more effort and energy into your workout you will likely get the results you are looking for sooner, especially with the right nutrition, motivation and mindset.

How much does it cost?

A session can be 45 minutes to 1 hour long and blocks of sessions can cost from £50-£150 depending on the type of workout and results you want. The cost of membership at the gym however is not included in the cost. Do get in touch with me to get an actual costing of your workout.
