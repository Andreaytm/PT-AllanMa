Your Workout Plan

In our first meeting consultation we meet up to discuss you:

  • your goals
  • what you want from your personal training sessions
  • what changes you wish to see in yourself physically and mentally
  • any potential challenges you can foresee how to mitigate these
  • and then we go over the short-term goals in order to achieve your transformation
  • and discuss a personalised plan of what you need to do to get there


This will form the basis of your personal workout plan and will be tailored to your needs, and around your life as much as possible.
Whatever you goals may be should it be getting beach beautiful, improving your health physically or mentally, improving fitness, training for a marathon or other, a plan will be made between us and will be your commitment to your fitness and health journey ahead.

My Transformation

In April of last year I set a challenge for myself, I cut down my intake of calories for 4 months and went from 76.6kg down to 70.8kg. Below is the result, the first picture was taken 19/4/16 and the second picture on 22/8/16. It took a lot of discipline, patience and focus to achieve this. I'm a massive food lover so going from a 4000 calorie daily diet slowly down to 1700-1800 was very tough. But if I can do it, anyone can! You just need to have the right mindset if you want to make a change. This of course was not nutrition alone I still kept up my workout programme, I went to the gym regularly and work out 5-6 days a week with a rest day each week.
