What is a Personal Trainer?

Personal trainers are certified professionals who can provide you advice, instructions and support on your fitness program and journey.
We provide mentoring, support, coaching and motivation in order for you to work to your potential to reach your fitness goals.
We have knowledge of human anatomy, functional exercise and basic nutrition.


Benefits of Having a Personal Trainer

Personal training is for everyone. It doesn't matter what age you are, your fitness level and ability or your body type.
A personal trainer provides you with an exercise and workout plan tailored to you.
Allowing you to reach your goals through a programme that works for you, your body, your time and your life:


  • Learn proper form and technique for safe training and to reduce injury and increase results
  • Establish a workout programme that works for you
  • Provide motivation and challenges for improvement and progression so you can work to your potential
  • Learn about the importance of stretching, hydration, breathing and good nutrition
  • Set and design realistic goals and expectations so that you can achieve and progress towards your ideal goal
